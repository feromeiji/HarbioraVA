About Habiora VA

Your trusted partner for modern virtual assistance and business support.

Company Identity

Habiora VA is a startup virtual assistance and business support agency composed of a small, agile team currently consisting of two founders. We specialize in providing remote operational, creative, and sales support for small businesses, startups, real estate professionals, and service-based companies primarily in international markets.

We operate with the belief that being small is an advantage—it allows us to be:

  • ✔ More flexible
  • ✔ More responsive
  • ✔ More personal
  • ✔ More adaptable to every client needs
  • ✔ Faster in execution compared to large agencies

Mission & Vision

Mission

To empower small businesses, startups, and real estate professionals by providing reliable, efficient, and high-quality virtual support that improves operations, increases productivity, and drives measurable business growth. We achieve this by acting as a seamless extension of our clients' teams while handling essential but time-consuming work with precision and care.

Vision

To become a globally trusted virtual assistance partner known for transforming small growing teams into highly efficient and scalable businesses through smart, human-centered operational support. We envision Habiora VA growing from a two-person startup into a globally recognized remote operations agency rooted in excellence, integrity, and measurable results.

Meet the Founders

The people behind Habiora VA.

Built by Passionate Professionals

Habiora VA is led by two founders committed to helping businesses become more organized, efficient, and scalable through dependable virtual assistance and operational support.

One founder brings legal training, a psychology background as a Registered Psychometrician in the Philippines, strong communication skills, structured workflow experience, and expertise in client relations and business operations.

Together, the founders combine operational discipline, adaptability, creativity, and a growth mindset to deliver exceptional service to every client.
