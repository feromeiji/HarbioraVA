Meet the Founders

The passionate professionals behind Habiora VA.

Built on Expertise, Growth, and Service

Habiora VA is founded by two professionals committed to helping businesses become more efficient through dependable virtual support.

One founder brings legal training, a psychology background as a Registered Psychometrician in the Philippines, and experience in communication, client intake, structured workflows, and business operations.

Together, the founders combine operational discipline, creativity, adaptability, and a commitment to delivering excellent client experiences.
